Animals-R-Us

Our Goal

Life as we know it becomes pretty stressful, and with social media becoming more and more awkward to navigate with the drama, social pressure, and negative posts and comments, we developed a simple site that only recreates pictures of cats or dogs. This removes all unnecessary sociatal pressure that develops around mean human interaction; you can just look at cat or dog pictures and decide whether you like it or not. Two birds with one stone! Voice your opinion without the negative social judgement.


We got cats!

There are a variety of cat images that you are able to like or dislike. What is there to miss? Meow meow.

We got dogs!

Don't be discouraged about our dog selection just because there is just one card about them! We have just as many dogs as we do cats with the same dislike/like function!

We got no drama!

With the lack of social interaction with other users, there is little drama!
